# 4WD-RC-Auto
4 wheel drive rc auto with bt remote

Hardware components:
1. Nodemcu ESP12-E dev board (based on esp8266). Datasheet in references.
2. Motor shield with 4 channels (basically two L293D motor drivers and one 74HC595 shift register). Datasheet in references.
3. 4 DC motors (connected to 4 wheels).

Planned:
1. two joysticks (each joystick has two potentiometer for x and y axis controll).
2. ESP32 liloin lite dev board.
